Dungeons & Dragons Spellbook Writer

This web app serves as both a searcher for the wide collection of spells in D&D, as well as a way for you to
create your own list of spells to use as a reference for your characters. By entering information about your
character, this app will automatically check to see which spells you can choose from, where you can then edit
and save a custom spell list that your character can use. In addition, you will be able to favorite select
spells that you frequently use for easy reference.
